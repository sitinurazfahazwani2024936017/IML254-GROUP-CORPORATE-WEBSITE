What type of cuisine does Aroma Malaya serve?

We specialize in authentic Malaysian cuisine, offering traditional dishes like Nasi Lemak, Satay, Laksa, and a variety of desserts and beverages inspired by local heritage.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes! We have several vegetarian dishes and can adjust certain recipes to suit vegan preferences. Please ask our staff for recommendations.

Can I make a reservation online?

Absolutely. You can book a table through our Reservation page, or contact us directly via phone or email for special requests.

Do you provide catering services?

Yes, Aroma Malaya offers catering for events, parties, and corporate functions. Contact us through the Promotion/Offers page for details.
