Our Story

From a humble Malaya kitchen to a growing chain of restaurants, Aroma Malaya preserves authentic spices and time-honored recipes.

A warm atmosphere blending tradition and modern comfort.

Roots & Journey

Starting as a small family-owned eatery, Aroma Malaya grew with a focus on fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, and heartfelt hospitality.

Family Origins

Founded by a couple inspired by their grandmother’s recipes from Kedah and Melaka—blending northern and southern influences into a unique identity.

Philosophy of Taste

Slow-roasted spices, fresh coconut milk, and sambal cooked to perfection— every dish preserves traditional techniques with care.
